## Why we built this · 缘起

作为科研工作者，一个最重要的职业理想应该是做出有影响力的高质量研究。然而当我仰望那些发在领域顶刊，引用数上千，对政策制定产生重要影响力的论文时，我脑海里浮现的第一个问题就是：

**这么好的 idea 是咋想出来的啊？**

于是，我们决定去问问这些写出高影响力论文的作者，了解一下这些重要论文的创作过程。

我们很感谢这些著名学者抽出宝贵的时间无私分享他们的创作经验，于是决定创立微信公众号Impactful Research，希望有更多学者可以从他们的分享中得到启发。

公众号于 2021 年 6 月开通；自 2026 年 4 月起，文章同步发布于本网站。

---

For a researcher, the most important career aspiration should be to produce impactful, high-quality work. Yet when I look up at the papers published in top field journals — cited thousands of times, shaping major policy decisions — the first question that comes to mind is:

**How did they come up with such a great idea?**

So we decided to ask the authors of these high-impact papers about how their work came to be.

We are deeply grateful to these distinguished scholars for taking the time to share their experience so generously, and we founded the WeChat official account *Impactful Research* in the hope that more researchers can find inspiration in what they have to say.

The WeChat official account launched in June 2021; as of April 2026, posts are also mirrored on this website.
