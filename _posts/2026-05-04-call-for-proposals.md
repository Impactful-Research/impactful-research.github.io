**好的经济学者们一直在做一件事：把好的研究讲清楚，把重要的问题讲明白。**

**Leading economists have long been engaged in one essential task: to make good research clear and to make important questions understandable.**

**但我们也逐渐意识到，仅仅解读论文还不够。很多真正关键的内容，其实来自于研究者本人的思考：为什么会做这个问题？ 这个研究背后的直觉是什么？ 他们如何看待这个领域未来的发展？**

**Yet, we have realized that simply interpreting papers to readers may not be not enough. Many of the most important insights do not fully appear in the paper itself, but rather come from the researcher’s own thinking: Why was this idea chosen? What is the intuition behind the research? How do they view the future of this field?**

**因此，Impactful Research 一直在做的事情是通过与学者的深度对话，持续解读研究的同时，呈现研究背后的思考过程与判断逻辑。**

**At Impactful Research, we aim to present the thinking process and judgment behind research through in-depth conversations with scholars.**

**而这一次，我们希望把采访权交给您。**

**Today, we invite you to take part.**

**一、我们在征集什么**

**I. What We Are Looking For**

**我们希望您提交一个采访提案（proposal），内容包括：**

**We invite you to submit an interview proposal, including:**

1.您想采访谁: 学者姓名 + 所在机构; 简要说明其研究方向或代表性工作

2\. 为什么是他 / 她, 我们更看重这一部分。请回答：您和他/她的学术交集？**这个学者“impactful”在哪里？** 他/她的研究对现实世界有什么重要意义？ 为什么现在值得做这次采访？

3\. 您想问什么问题, 请提供 5–8 个核心问题，问题需要体现：**对研究本身的理解（不是泛泛提问）;对机制/方法/影响的思考; 有潜力引出“论文之外”的内容**

4\. 您将如何完成这次采访, 简单说明：您是否有相关学术背景；您预计如何推进采访（线上/面对面等）

我们将从选题判断力、问题质量和执行能力等角度进行筛选。

1.Who you would like to interview? Name of the scholar and affiliation ; A brief introduction to their research area or representative work

2.Why this scholar? This is the most important part. Please address: What is your academic connection (if any) with the scholar? **In what sense is him/her “impactful”?** What is the real-world significance of his/her research? Why is now the right time for this interview?

3.What questions you would ask? Please provide 5–8 core questions that demonstrate: **Understanding of his/her research; Your thoughts on mechanisms, methods, and implications; your potential to elicit insights beyond the paper**

4.How you will conduct the interview? Please Briefly describe your relevant background; Your plan for conducting the interview (e.g., online or in person)

We will evaluate proposals based on topic, question quality, and execution ability.

**二．协作须知（请务必阅读）**

**II. Collaboration Guidelines (Please Read Carefully)**

**为了保证采访质量，以及避免对学者造成不必要的打扰，共建高效合作流程，敬请共同遵守以下约定：**

**To ensure interview quality and avoid unnecessary requests to scholars, we kindly ask all participants to follow these guidelines:**

**1\. 提案先行审核**

· 所有采访提案须经Impactful Research编辑团队审核确认后，方可使用平台名义联系学者。

· 未经审核自行对接的提案，暂不纳入合作范围。

**2\. 筛选通过后开展对接**

· 提案入选后，我们将共同确定最终采访方案，编辑部同步提供学者沟通建议，协助推进对接工作。

**3\. 全程沟通同步留痕**

· 与学者往来的邀约邮件及后续沟通，均需抄送官方邮箱：📮impactful.research.blog@gmail.com，保持流程透明规范，相关要求我们也将同步告知受访学者。

**4\. 统一授权规范使用**

· 未经平台授权，请勿私自以本公众号名义开展采访及对外联络。

· 所有采访内容，需经编辑团队审核后方可发布。

**1.Proposal must be approved before outreach**

· All proposals must be reviewed and approved by the Impactful Research editorial team before you contact any scholar on behalf of the platform.

· Proposals without prior approval will not be considered.

**2.Outreach after approval**

· Once selected, we will jointly finalize the interview plan. The editorial team will provide guidance on communication and assist when necessary

**3.Transparency**

· All invitation emails and follow-up correspondence with the scholar must cc our official email:  
📮impactful.research.blog@gmail.com

·This ensures a transparent and standardized communication process (we will also inform the interviewee accordingly)

**4.Proper use of the platform name**

· Please do not use the name of Impactful Research for any outreach without authorization

· All interview content will be reviewed by the editorial team before publication

**三、合作支持**

**III. What We Provide**

**对于入选的提案，我们将提供：**

**For selected proposals, we will offer:**

· 采访框架与问题优化建议

· 与学者沟通的支持，必要时由编辑团队协助

· 后期内容编辑与发布

· 在 Impactful Research 平台署名发表，面向超过 12,000 名读者传播

· Guidance on interview structure and question design

· Support in communicating with the scholar

· Editing and publication support

· Publication under your name on the Impactful Research platform, reaching over 12,000 subscribers with interests
